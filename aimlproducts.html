Smart Document Search
Smart Document Search is an AI-powered assistant for instantly locating precise answers in business documents like contracts, reports, and emails. Contract Analysis
Contract analysis uses AI to read and summarize legal contracts, highlighting key clauses, obligations, terms, and risks. It flags problematic or non-compliant clauses early, ensuring policy and regulatory compliance. By delivering fast, clear insights, it accelerates negotiations, empowers better decision-making, and frees legal teams from manual review, allowing focus on higher-value strategic work.

Invoice Extraction
Invoice extraction automates the capture of key details from invoices in any format, including scanned PDFs, images, and digital files.
